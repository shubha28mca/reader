Why pay alone fails in high-stress nursing jobs

1. Burnout is primarily caused by workload, not income.
Long shifts, understaffing, constant emotional pressure, and unpredictable emergencies drain nurses faster than a salary increase can motivate them. Chronic fatigue, compassion fatigue, and sleep disruption aren’t solved by a higher paycheck.
